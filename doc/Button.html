Buttons are meant to direct users into taking certain actions. Different types of buttons create a hierarchy that helps users navigate and complete actions.
Buttons allow users to take actions, and make choices, with a single tap.Buttons communicate actions that users can take. There are different type of buttons - primary button , disabled button and CTA button. A "floating action button" is a circular icon button that hovers over content to promote a primary action in the application. Floating action buttons are most commonly used in chatbots, notifications or any buttons that are fixed on the right bottom side and scroll when the page is scrolling.
